Infusion and Distillation

  1. Combine the following ingredients in 800 ml of 43% Alcohol and steep for 10 days:
    • Wormwood - 28g
    • Hyssop - 9g
    • Calamus - 17g
    • Melissa - 6g
    • Aniseed - 28g
    • Fennel - 25g
    • Star Anise - 10g
    • Coriander - 3g
  2. Add 600ml of Distilled or Reverse Osmosis Filtered Water, steep for an additional 3 days.
  3. Filter out solids from the macerated liquid and distill.
  4. Dilute the recovered Distillate to 50% with Distilled Water or Reverse Osmosis Filtered Water.

Coloring

Green Absinthe

  1. Combine the following in the 50% Diluted Distillate, steep for 3 to 4 days:
    • Mint - 4g
    • Melissa - 1g
    • Wormwood - 3g
    • Citrus Peel - 1g
    • Liquorice (Glycyrrhiza Glabra Root) - 4g

Red Absinthe

  1. Combine the following in the 50% Diluted Distillate, steep for 3 to 4 days:
    • Hibiscus - 28g
    • Orange Zest - 9g
    • Tarragon - 5g

Final Steps

  1. Filter through multiple coffee filters to remove solids and sediment.
  2. Bottle the absinthe.
  3. Consume with sugar, 1 cube per 25 ml tot, and diluted with cold water.
